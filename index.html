A little about me

Welcome to Phoebe Tang's professional portfolio. I am a trilingual costume designer and art historian, specialising in the fusion of historical insights and fashion. I hold a Master’s degree in Historical Costume from the Arts University Bournemouth and a Bachelor’s in Fine Art from the University of Hong Kong. I worked as a Costume Designer and Costume Supervisor in theatre productions. In 2024, my costume design was exhibited in the “Fashioning Our World” Exhibition at the Salisbury Museum. Recently, I have been authoring my research book on the costume history of the 20th-century aviation industry. My work is driven by a philosophy that embraces cross-cultural elements, enhancing storytelling while respecting diverse traditions. Thank you for exploring my journey in costume design and art history.
